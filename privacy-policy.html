Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 22 June, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Robotics NLK. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

Robotics NLK does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive data. The app works entirely offline and does not require access to personal information.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with or transmit any data to third-party services.

Children’s Privacy

Because the app collects no data, it is safe for all age groups, including children.

User Rights & Data Removal Requests

All files you create inside Robotics NLK are stored locally on your device in the App’s Documents folder.

You can:

  • Review your files using the Files tab inside the app.
  • Delete any files manually.
  • Delete the app to remove all stored data.

If you enable iCloud sync, data is transferred through your personal iCloud account. Only devices signed in with your Apple ID have access to this data. You may review Apple’s privacy policy here: Apple Privacy Policy

If you enable Local Network transfer, data moves only between devices on your Wi-Fi network with Robotics NLK installed. Learn more about local network privacy here: TN3179: Understanding Local Network Privacy .

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
