User Rights & Data Removal Requests

All your recorded data is stored locally on your device - in Documents folder of the app. You can review all data stored going to Files tab and remove the files there. Or all data will be deleted by removing the app from your device.

In the app you'll be able to enable iCloud data transfer between your devices. All the data will be transferred through your iCloud account. Apple makes sure that only you and your devices with your Apple Id can access that data. The iCloud Privacy Policy can be reviewed Apple Privacy Policy.

In the app you'll be able to enable the Local Network data transfer between the devices with Robotics NLK installed. For details on the local network privacy review the following article TN3179: Understanding local network privacy.
