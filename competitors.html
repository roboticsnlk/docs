Here you'll find a short basic competitor analysis of our product grouped by features.

GEOINT Maps Comparison with position Sync

  • No Competitors found at AppStore On iPad OS you can open several windows - but the maps positions won't get synced automatically. So you'll need to reposition each of the viewed maps manually. In Robotics NLK you are able to open several map options - and sync them. So that if the region of interest changes on one map - it will change on others as well.

Map Tiles Caching and available Offline

  • MAPS.ME – Offline maps & navigation Available on iOS (and Android) and uses OSM data.
  • OsmAnd – Maps & GPS Offline OpenStreetMap based and supports offline map downloads.
