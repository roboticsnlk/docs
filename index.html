Robotics NLK

Robotics NLK is an iOS/iPadOS app letting you configure custom missions, relying on iPhone/iPad sensors.
The goal is to get maximum out of your iOS device, with maximum flexibility and configurability.
In the nearest future it will be possible to order BLE/WiFi accessories making your missions even more powerful, transforming your mobile device into a robot.

Features

  • Synced Maps
    - Compare Maps keeping them in Sync - while moving position on one map - synced maps will move as well. GEOINT tool.
    - Support for default maps as well as OpenStreetMap, OpenTopoMap - with caching mechanism - so that cached map tiles are operational offline.
  • Configurable Tabata Timer
    - Super flexible Tabata Timer - with actions like pronouncing phrases, or vibrating. Configured as Nodes.
    - This lets you setup any custom workout with voice commands guidance.
  • GPS Track Recorder
    - Record your GPS Track, Run Track, Hike Track. The GeoJSON is recorded.
    - Export or View the GeoJSON track over the Map.
    - Visualise the tracks as a beautiful FlyOver Video for sharing.
  • iCloud Baby Monitor / Security Camera
    - Capture slideshow images on first device with your iCloud active.
    - Read slideshow images using another device with your iCloud active. Syncs approximately each 10 seconds.
    - Secure data transfer via your iCloud between your devices. No external servers get access to your data.
  • WiFi/BLE Baby Monitor / Security Camera
    - Capture slideshow images on one device, which will broadcast it via your Local Network.
    - Receive slideshow images on another device in the same Local Network. Syncs approximately each second.
    - Secure data transfer via your Local Network between several RNLK app installations.
  • REST API/cURL Calls
    - Call any url at any event in the mission - by triggering a cURL.
